Ph.D Candidate & Assistant Professor

Currently I am a Ph.D. candidate in the State Key Laboratory of Information Security, Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Xiaochun Cao. And I am also a assistant professor in School of Information Engineering, Tianjin University of Commerce. My current research interests include community detection, machine learning and computer vision.
